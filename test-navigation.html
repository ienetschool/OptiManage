You can also access these pages directly:

Troubleshooting:

If you don't see these menu items:

  1. Make sure you're logged in to the application
  2. Click on "Patient Management" in the sidebar to expand it
  3. Look for the shopping cart 🛒 and settings ⚙️ icons
  4. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+R)
